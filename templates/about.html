Welcome!



Welcome to the site! Helping you find the bank that is right for you. Many guides and services exist to help you go about selecting a bank. Sites such as moneysavingexpert.com exist telling you what the best banking products on offer are. However, no site exists that combines the two and tries to help you select a bank that fits all of your needs as a consumer not just for a particular service. This is where this site comes in.

The site aims to make the process easier for you by having all the information that you need all on one site taking away that tedious activity of having to search the internet for hours and reading a multitude of articles.

The site has combined information and data from a variety of sources to create a guide to how to select a bank and to create a search algorithm to help match you to a bank. Sites like this exist for services such as insurance with GoCompare.com being a good example, but not for banking.

The bank data has been sourced from reliable and consistent sources such as the bank sites themselves, moneysavingexpert.com, and studies produced by Ipsos. The site is free from bias as the goal is to satisfy the needs of the user.

We hope that all the information you require to make your decision can be sourced from our site greatly reducing your search time whilst providing you with more in-depth research than one would normally undertake during the process.

Please have a read of our guides and use the terminology dictionary to familiarise yourself with the process of selecting a bank and increasing your banking and financial knowledge. Following this, the search algorithm will provide you with the banks that provide the best match for what you desire. Using these results, you can easily access the recommended banks and continue your last bits of research before coming to your final decision.
