An intro to the site

Our goal is to help you find the bank most suited to you as person, not just the services a bank provides. Banking is a huge part of our day to day lives and so it is important your bank fulfills all of your needs.

The site includes a search algorithm that uses your answers to search for a matching bank as well as guides to help you through the process of finding your new bank.
