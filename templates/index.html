An intro to the site

This site aims to guide you through the process of selecting the right bank for you. The relationship between banks and their consumers is not always the strongest. Banks heavily focus on trying to sell you a product as they should, however, their is not always the quality of care to go with it. Different banking consumers have different needs. Jut because a bank provides you with the best offer for a product does not always mean it is the best option for you as an individual with your personal needs.

For a more detailed explanation into this please look at our guides and about us pages on the site. These pages will educate you on what you should look at for when selecting a bank, the areas to be aware of, why a bank might not be right for you and hopefully expand your knowledge around the banking world helping you make better decisions for the bank you decide to join next.
